About Us

What This Program is For

Atrium Health™ Medical Learning is designed to provide a structured, self-paced educational experience for students interested in medical knowledge and clinical skills. Our program offers practical insights into healthcare concepts while fostering critical thinking and self-directed learning.

What We Do

We provide a 4-month intensive self-learning program covering essential medical topics, including anatomy, physiology, medical terminology, pathophysiology, pharmacology, clinical skills, ethics, and specialty care. Students progress through modules, complete quizzes, earn badges, and track their learning milestones.

What We Strive to Do

Our mission is to cultivate knowledgeable, skilled, and ethical future healthcare professionals. We strive to create an engaging, accessible, and interactive learning environment that empowers students to achieve mastery in medical knowledge and prepares them for real-world healthcare challenges.
